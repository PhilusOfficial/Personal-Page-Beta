About Me

Hello, folks!
As you probably have noticed by visiting the home page, my name is Federico Lucifora. Why do I call myself a web surfer, you say? Well, it’s pretty simple. I currently have no job to brag about, and just writing that I’m a student isn’t really exciting. So, I told myself, what is that one word that describes the best what I do? The answer is straightforward: I have different passions and hobbies, but none of them really prevails over the others; but there is one thing they have in common, and that is that all of them origin from the internet. These are the words then: web surfer.

I’m currently a student of Communication Design at Politecnico di Milano. I previously attended Liceo Scientifico Filippo Lussana, in Bergamo, where I got my high school diploma.
I’m involved in different activities: I create custom videogame levels for the game Mario Kart Wii, called custom tracks. I started making them when I was in middle school, which let me reach a high level of knowledge of the English language, since the community of custom track creators is mainly an English-speaking community. I also have a YouTube channel where I seldom upload comedy videos. I also take photos for personal use or for small companies. I’m currently working on the backstage of a horror movie made by the company “Mafarka Film”; the photos are unavailable since the movie has yet to be released. Finally, I learned how to perform magic tricks with cards through the internet. I often try to surprise my friends and the people I meet with them.

So yeah, that’s it. This sums up the important things that need to be said in a personal page, or at least that’s what I think is enough. Want to learn some more? Visit this page, where I talk more in depth about my skills.
